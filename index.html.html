FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How long does setup take?
−
About 30 minutes. You add your agents (bulk Excel upload or one by one), set up your process, and you're auditing. No IT team, no integrations, no training sessions needed.
Can I use my own scoring parameters?
−
Yes — on the Enterprise plan, scoring parameters are fully customizable per client. On Starter and Growth, the current parameters cover the most common BPO QA frameworks and work for most teams out of the box.
Where is my data stored? Is it secure?
−
All data is stored on Google Firebase (Asia Southeast region), with role-based authentication. Only users you explicitly assign can access your process data. Your data is completely isolated from other clients.
What's included in the 60-day free trial?
−
Full access to everything — all analytics, all features, unlimited audits. No credit card needed. We give you 60 days because we know QA results take time to show up in the data. You'll see the difference before you pay anything.
How many agents and auditors can I add?
−
Starter supports up to 50 agents and 5 QA auditors. Growth supports unlimited agents and 15 auditors. Enterprise has no limits on either.
Do my TLs and agents get their own login?
−
Yes. Role-based access means admins, QA auditors, and team leaders each see a different version of the platform appropriate to their role. TLs see their team's data. Agents can view their own scores. Admins see everything.
Can I export data to share with my client?
−
Yes — one-click Excel export for audit logs, performance data, and pareto analysis. The format is clean and professional — ready to send to a client or present in a review meeting.
